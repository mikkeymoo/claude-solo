Capture this idea as a structured brief in `.planning/BRIEF.md`.

Structure:

1. **Problem** — what pain does this solve? One sentence.
2. **Goal** — what does success look like? One sentence.
3. **Scope** — what's in? What's explicitly out?
4. **Users** — who uses this? How often?
5. **Constraints** — tech stack, deadlines, compatibility requirements
6. **Open questions** — things that need answering before work starts

Keep it tight. No fluff. If I gave you a vague idea, ask the 2-3 most important clarifying questions before writing anything.

End with: "Brief saved. Ready to /mm:plan?"
